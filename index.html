About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Smith Institute for Political Economy and Philosophy at Chapman University.

I work in Philosophy of Science, PPE, and Business Ethics. My research lies at the intersection of philosophy and economics and is primarily focused on the epistemic capacities of scientific models and the role of expectations and uncertainty in economic thought. I also work on interdisciplinary normative projects concerning the rights and obligations of business leaders and regulators.

Prior to coming to Chapman University, I earned my PhD in Philosophy and an MA in Economics from Duke University. Before that, I earned an MA in Philosophy from University College London and a BSc in Philosophy from the London School of Economics.

Feel free to contact me at finestone@chapman.edu.
