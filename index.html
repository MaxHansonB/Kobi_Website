About

I am a doctoral candidate at Duke University. I work in Philosophy of Science, PPE, and Business Ethics. My research lies at the intersection of philosophy and economics and is primarily focused on the epistemic capacities of scientific models and the role of expectations and uncertainty in economic thought. I also work on interdisciplinary normative projects concerning the rights and obligations of business leaders and regulators.

Feel free to contact me at kobi.finestone@duke.edu.
